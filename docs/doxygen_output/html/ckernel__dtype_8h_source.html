14  *
15  * Simple quantization:

18  *
19  * GGML-compatible quantization (block-based with scales):
20  * - CK_DT_Q4_0: 4-bit, 32 weights/block, 1 FP16 scale
21  * - CK_DT_Q4_K: 4-bit k-quant, 256 weights/block, nested scales (Q4_K_M)
